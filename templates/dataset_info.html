Training Dataset

The HFTL model trained and used in this application involves 997 plant species from the PlantCLEF 2021 dataset. It is currently the largest benchmark dataset for cross-domain plant identification comprising both herbarium and field training images. However, note that only a subset of species have herbarium-field data (as in real-world scenarios), resulting in a lower prediction accuracy for the species with only herbarium data. More research works are yet to be explored to tackle this problem.
